# Company Profile
Once you have filled your company profile, you can start posting job ads instantly. To do this, you need to create a job ad. You can create a job ad by clicking on the **Create Job Ad** button on the top right corner of the page.

```
https://www.videojobs.io/company-page-url
```

The company profile can be used as a canvas to showcase your companyâ€™s culture, values, and mission. It shows the collection of job ads that are posted by the company.

## Predefined Information
The following information can be changed from the [Account](/account/account-page) page:

 - Company Profile Image
 - Company Name
 - Company Page URL
 - Company Website
 - Location
 - Team Size
 - Bio
 - Mission
